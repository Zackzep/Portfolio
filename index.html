About Me

I am a graduate of a two-year computer science program with a strong foundation in object-oriented programming, control structures, MVC architecture, procedural programming, and an emphasis on writing clean, efficient code. My passion for coding developed when I realized the similarities between the structure of code and symbolic logic. As a philosophy graduate who enjoyed logic-based courses, I found this connection exciting and saw computer science as a natural career choice. Given the importance of technology going forward, it felt like the perfect fit for my skills and interests.

Here, on a webpage designed by me, you'll find projects I have worked on or am currently developing. I hope you find them interesting!
